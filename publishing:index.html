The House

A Legacy Built
One Word at a Time

Sturm & Róisín Publishing was born from two convictions: that every story deserves a home worthy of its weight, and that small presses can do extraordinary things when built with intention.

The name itself holds the mission. Sturm — force, momentum, impact. Róisín — grace, innocence, enduring beauty. Together they describe the full range of human expression we set out to honor.

We are a family-founded independent publisher operating out of Delight, Arkansas. Our six imprints span children's books to military fiction, from quiet memoir to deep nonfiction — because legacy doesn't come in a single shape.

"Words are not merely recorded here. They are given ground to stand on."

Kriecher Imprint
Found by Obituary:
A Dead Reckoning
By Christopher James Heiller

At thirty-three, Christopher James Heiller discovered that the father he'd believed dead — killed in a motorcycle accident — was alive. His father found him. What followed wasn't merely reunion — it was recalibration, and the beginning of understanding a life already lived.

Dead reckoning is how navigators find their position without landmarks — using only where they've been, how fast they've moved, and what direction they've traveled. For thirty-three years, that's exactly what Christopher was doing. Moving forward. Making sense of himself with the information he had. He had been a Heiller before he ever knew the name. The bloodline, the instincts, the questions that never quite had answers — all of it suddenly had coordinates. He didn't find his father. His father found him. And in being found, he finally understood where he'd been going all along.
